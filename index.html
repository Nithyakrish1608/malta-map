MALTA MAP

VALLETTA

Valletta is the capital of Malta, on the northeast coast of the island of Malta.

The smallest capital city in the entire European Union territory.

Area: 80 ha

BIRKIRKARA

Birkirkara is a city in the central region of Malta.

Birkirkara means "cold water" or "running water". This is attributed to the valley in the town.

Area: 2.7 km²

ZEJTUN

Żejtun is a city in the South Eastern Region of Malta

Żejtun is traditionally known as Città Beland

Area: 5.37 km²

MOSTA

Mosta is a small but densely populated city in the Northern Region of Malta.

Area: 6.8 km²

COMINO

Comino, formerly called Ephaestia is a small island of the Maltese archipelago between the islands of Malta and Gozo in the Mediterranean Sea.

Area: 3.5 km²
